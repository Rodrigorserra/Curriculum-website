Projects

During my degree in Biomedical Science, I participated in a project, in the discipline of Programming, named "Timed Up and Go test (TUG)".

A group with different physical conditions was subjected to activities (running, walking, jumping) always linked to an ECG. Through MATLAB programming, the information collected from the information provided by the patient and the data collected by signals and mobility analysis, using EMG e ECG, was monitored. This project aimed to monitor sedentary lifestyles in Portugal.
